What is this site about?

Every gamer knows the feeling when looking for a good group to have fun in games. The matchmaking built into most games simply has no settings that would allow a targeted search. Most of the time, the first best players are rolled together into a team and they have to get along with each other.


With GamerPals this feeling belongs to history. We offer targeted filters and an active community, so you can always find a team that would like to play a few more rounds.
